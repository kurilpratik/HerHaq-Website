Our Mission is to
Empower Women

Her Haq is a youth led not-for-profit organization that works towards gender equality and women empowerment. It is the successor of the student collective, Girl Up Rise (a UN Foundation initiative). We wish to create an ideal world where no woman has to fight to make her voice heard.

Here is how we
help women

Her Haq has three main pillars - menstrual hygiene management, legal literacy, and financial literacy. Through our multiple campaigns including #SpotTheStigma we have been able to create impact across India.
